The Plan

homeagenda

Investment Climate

The progress of Nigerian businesses will remain a priority.

Yemi Osinbajo is committed to ensuring that everyone doing business in Nigeria, from multinationals, large corporations and established businesses to tech start-ups, and small businesses, is able to do so seamlessly and with ease.


He will:

  • Intensify and expand existing reform efforts in order to ensure that the regulatory compliance by businesses are cost-effective, speedy and less cumbersome.
  • Digitize the application processes of business regulatory agencies for greater efficiency.

Making our courts work for businesses:


Yemi Osinbajo will:

  • Expand the pool of available judges by appointing more judges to clear backlog cases, and boosting the remuneration and benefits of judicial officers and staff;
  • Work with State Governments to ensure that small claims are addressed expeditiously across all the States of the Federation;
  • Work with the judiciary to accelerate the adoption of digital technology in our courts for easier and quicker resolution of commercial disputes;

Championing MSMEs


Yemi Osinbajo will:

  • Guarantee that MSMEs have access to finance;
  • Provide MSMEs with the machinery and equipment they need by expanding MSMEs shared facilities across all the states in Nigeria;
  • Facilitate a good rapport between MSMEs and regulatory agencies through MSME clinics and one-stop shops.

To rapidly develop Nigeria and truly make her great again, start by choosing a better leader.
