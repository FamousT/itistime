Osinbajo's Leadership Agenda

If elected into office as the next president of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, Osinbajo will continue and consolidate the reforms and policies started by President Muhammadu Buhari.



Improving our Livelihood



Osinbajo will continue to implement reforms and policies that will improve the livelihood of Nigerians and of our country as a whole. He will improve the livelihood of Nigerians by facilitating the creation of millions of new and better jobs. He will implement policies to strengthen our economy to create prosperity for all.



Improving Our Livelihood - Agriculture



Osinbajo will consolidate on the gains made so far in the Agriculture sector by: Improving the efficiency and sufficiency of domestic food production to ensure that we create jobs for our people and eat what we grow; and Supporting the Agriculture sector to ensure that Nigeria becomes a net exporter of food.



Investing in our People - Skills



Osinbajo will continue to empower our people to take on today and tomorrow's jobs, by: Investing in improving the skills of our existing workforce; and Attracting and incentivizing the best talent, and prioritizing continuous learning and high standards.



Investing in our People - Education



Osinbajo will sustain the transformation of the Education sector by: Improving the quality of education available in our schools; and Structuring the education system to develop skills that are key to nation building such as hard work, discipline, cooperation, unity, respect, honesty, service, leadership, accountability, and integrity.



Investing in Our People - Social Security



Osinbajo will also continue to empower our people by: Deepening and expanding the existing Social Investment Programme for greater impact and ensuring that no one is left behind.



Improving our Infrastructure



Osinbajo will also continue to ensure the seamless production and movement of goods and services within Nigeria by: Continuing to develop and improve our transport infrastructure - roads, rail, airports and seaports; and Generating and distributing sufficient energy to power our homes and industries by exploiting our natural gas reserves.



Investing in Our People - Healthcare



Osinbajo will consolidate on the reforms made in the Healthcare sector by: Improving and equipping our hospitals to meet global standards and cater for our people; Improving access to healthcare for all Nigerians especially at the grassroot level by strengthening our primary healthcare system; and Incentivizing, training and retraining of health workers to ensure optimum and uninterrupted service delivery.



Progressing Our Nation


Osinbajo will also continue efforts of President Muhammadu Buhari to move our nation forward by: Implementing policies that will strengthen our bond of unity and instil a sense of patriotism in Nigerians across board; Reforming our civil service by empowering it with the capacity to implement government policies efficiently and effectively; and Promoting greater women participation in political and business Leadership and ensuring greater financial inclusion for our women.



Ensuring Peace and Security



Osinbajo will also continue the efforts of President Muhammadu Buhari in prioritising security, law and order in Nigeria by: ensuring that our law enforcement agencies and armed forces are sufficiently remunerated, well resourced, and adequately trained to protect and defend our country.



To rapidly develop Nigeria and truly make her great again, start by choosing a better leader.
