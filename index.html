MAN WITH THE PEOPLE


Yemi Osinbajo; the man with the people



The Plan

A Unified Country

A country divided will not stand. Yemi Osinbajo will unify the nation as one cohesive yet diverse unit. People will not be discriminated against on the basis of ethnicity, religion, gender.



Yemi Osinbajo will:

Promote inclusion in decision making, allocation of opportunities and participation;

Promote equity in the allocation of state resources;

Adopt and propagate a national ethos that projects the Nigerian spirit and a body of similar attitudes and positive cultural values beneficial to the nation

Galvanise government, civil society, private sector, faith-based organisations and traditional institutions to advance the cohesion and integration agenda;

Restore confidence in government by promoting values of integrity in public service;

Ensure justice and promote healing and restoration for injured communities and people.


Investing in People


Yemi Osinbajo will Intentionally invest in the development of practical, creative and innovative skills for young Nigerians that will be the catalyst for Nigeria’s emerging knowledge economy.

Jobs and Opportunity


With a large youth population, Nigeria is in a strong position to build a workforce that is skilled, competent and successful.



To do this, Yemi Osinbajo will prioritise the Manufacturing, Technology, Agriculture and Construction sectors.

In prioritising these sectors, Yemi Osinbajo will focus on:

Supporting young people to acquire relevant skills;

Building infrastructure to support the prioritised sectors;

Creating a supportive business environment; and

Where necessary, provide or facilitate financing for businesses and young people to thrive in entrepreneurship;

Promoting integrated rural development

A Safe Country For All

Yemi Osinbajo will continue to invest in building the capacity of our security forces and win the battle against banditry, terrorism, extremism, kidnapping and other anarchical enterprises.



Yemi Osinbajo will:

Enact a modern and relevant security architecture and framework that prioritises and addresses Nigeria's current security realities.

Address both human and material resources constraints of the Nigerian military and police force.

Address the Farmer-Pastoralist conflict through the continued and full implementation of the National Livestock Transformational Plan.

Actively engage the regional, continental and global dimensions of the issues that add pressure to Nigeria’s insecurity concerns.

Advance ongoing efforts in police reform to establish a modern 21st century Police.

To securing our communities – ensure decentralised policing and community policing approaches

Boost the military Industrial Capacity with a view to making it a market leader on the African continent.


An Inclusive Society

Yemi Osinbajo recognises that an inclusive society is important for sustainable prosperity and will ensure the review of existing legislations and policies to eliminate systemic discrimination against vulnerable groups.


He will:

Ensure that women and young people fill a minimum of 35% of appointive positions;

Work with organised private sector to ensure that women represent 35% of leadership positions in the private sector;


Foreign Policy

Yemi Osinbajo will maintain Nigeria’s leadership position within Africa and work to deepen Nigeria’s role in regional integration. Through specific policy and value chain support, he will improve our comparative advantage to position Nigerian businesses to capture a significant portion of the African market.

Infrastructure

Over the last 7 years, there has been an unprecedented investment in Nigeria’s infrastructure. Yemi Osinbajo will continue these enabling investments until the whole of Nigeria is completely integrated, such that people, goods and services can flow freely within, as well as to and from Nigeria.

To be competitive, the economy must be a connected one. Yemi Osinbajo will consolidate upon the progress and expand Nigeria’s infrastructure to support, strengthen both hard and virtual connectivity through the following means:

Rail: Expand upon the Lagos-Ibadan Standard Gauge and the Abuja-Kaduna rail line by actualizing a strategic rail development that connects all major economic centres across Nigeria. Key priority areas will be completion of the Lagos- Kano and Lagos- Calabar rail projects,

Roads & Bridges: Continue to utilise innovative mechanisms like the Sukuk Bonds to expand our roads and bridges network.

Airports: We will improve the maintenance of and boost the operational efficiency of our four major international airports through the swift concessioning of - Abuja, Lagos, Port Harcourt and Kano airports in the next four years.

Seaports: Improve efficiency at Apapa ports, especially for export activity. Open up existing ports in the South East and South South regions of the country to ease exports.

Broadband Infrastructure: Increase broadband penetration by 20% annually over the next 4 years;

Achieving Energy Security for Nigeria


Yemi Osinbajo will improve our energy efficiency by optimising our current installed capacity and diversifying our energy mix. He will harness natural gas reserves as a transitional energy source as both feedstock and energy source for industry.

Yemi Osinbajo will:

Improve our energy efficiency by optimising our current installed capacity:

Harness natural gas reserves as a transitional energy source as both feedstock and energy source for industry;

Diversify our energy mix and expand energy access by including greater use of solar energy through the implementation of the Solar Naija, Energising Education and Energising Economies Programmes;

Solve the persistent issue of National Grid collapse by expanding the use of interconnected minigrids;

Solve domestic fuel shortage by building national reserves

Eliminate sabotage of hydrocarbon and power infrastructure

Investment Climate


The progress of Nigerian businesses will remain a priority. Yemi Osinbajo is committed to ensuring that everyone doing business in Nigeria, from multinationals, large corporations and established businesses to tech start-ups, and small businesses, is able to do so seamlessly and with ease.


He will:

Intensify and expand existing reform efforts in order to ensure that the regulatory compliance by businesses are cost-effective, speedy and less cumbersome.

Digitize the application processes of business regulatory agencies for greater efficiency.
